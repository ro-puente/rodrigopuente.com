Multiplaza - Grupo Roble

This project is a custom Ruby on Rails CMS with a twist.

Every single piece of information uploaded to this app feeds an AI recommender algorithm that delivers user's custom content they will probably like to interact with.

This is one of the biggest systems I've had to develop and mantain. The app works for seven malls in six different countries in latin america, with an average of 100,000 monthly sessions and about 450,000 monthly pageviews

The app uses a Russian Doll cache implementation on a Redis store, along with Sidekiq background jobs for the recommender algorithm and Elastic Search for full text search. The app is hosted on Heroku and uses Amazon S3 and Amazon Cloudfront to deliver every asset it needs.

Pilsener 110 años

Industrias La Constancia wanted to celebrate their 110th aniversary, so they decided to give away 110 flight tickets so their clients could bring any family member anywhere in the world to toast with the most iconic beer brand in El Salvador by collecting corcholatas as currency.

This Ruby on Rails app was actually composed by three smaller modules. One was for Pilsener customers, where they could create a campaign to bring their friends home and also check real-time stats to check how they were doing compared to other users. The second one was for every support center, where they kept track of every corcholata their customer deposited on their virtual account. The third one was a custom analytics dashboard where brand managers kept track of important key metrics to assure everything was running on track for this project.

The campaign gave away the 110 tickets and bring customers from over 27 different countries, collecting over half a million corcholatas, being a total success.
